About Us

Educate • Empower • Inspire

SheEqual is a Youth lead organization working to promote Women and Girls Rights in Bangladesh through different development activities, Storytelling, Research, Volunteering, Raising awareness, Campaign design, organizing capacity building training and workshops for the youth as well as the community people. We also circulate the women rights related laws, remarkable judgments and success stories within the community along with the digital spaces. We are very much committed to continue a promising advocacy for combating gender stereotypes and advancement of Women and Girls Rights in Bangladesh.
