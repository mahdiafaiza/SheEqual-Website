Shahriar Bin Wares

Campaign Manager

Furthermore, Shahriar has an expertise on article writing in respect of human rights and legal system of Bangladesh, as a result many of his articles has published in various English newspapers such as Daily observer, Finally, he is about to start his postgraduate diploma in law in the UK. Following this, he is determined to study Bar at Law and aims to be a Barrister. In a nutshell, he focuses to establish his career in legal profession and wants to work for the underprivileged community with regard to ensuring their Human Rights.
